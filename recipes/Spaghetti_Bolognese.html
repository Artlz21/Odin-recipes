Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Bolognese is a rich savory meat sauce that is the perfect comfort food. It can be used with a variaty of pastas but fits perfectly with spaghetti :).

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1/4 tsp Chili Flakes
  • Olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Red wine (Dry wine is best, Merlot is a good choice.)
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 can of peeled tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 tbsn tomato paste
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Steps

  1. First is prep, start by dicing the onion and carrot into small pieces and set aside. Smash and peel the garlic but keep it whole Measure and set aside all other ingredients from the list.
  2. Pour a generous amount of oil to a heated pan and add the oil. When the oil is ready raise to a medium high heat and add the onion and carrot and heat until softened.
  3. Add garlic and Chili flakes and stir till fragrent, then add ground beef breaking up as you stir, cook until browned.
  4. Add wine and reduce heat to low medium and let the wine reduce for about 15 min.
  5. Add can of peeled tomatoes, fill with water can and add to pot. Smash down and stir tomatoes then add tomato paste. Leave uncovered and reduce for 15-20 min
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste and leave covered on lowest heat. Start pot of boiling water, once is brought to a rolling boil add salt then add pasta and cook according to instructions of box.
